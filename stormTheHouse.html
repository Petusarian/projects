Enhanced Browser Shooter

Protect your wall from waves of enemies!

Controls:
Click anywhere to shoot. Instead of visible bullets, you'll see a brief impact effect at the mouse position.
If you click on an enemy, it will rapidly shake and disappear (simulate a quick death).
Ammo is displayed as a bar (with numbers) in the top-left and reloads automatically.

Enemies approach from the left, stop at your wall, and attack it with a punch-like animation.
If your wall’s health reaches 0, it's game over.
